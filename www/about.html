About AA Rescue Service

AA Rescue Service is a suite of mobile phone applications designed to give you critical help on the road. The suite currently comprises:

AA Rescue: A roadside emergency alert button that, as registered user, sends your name, AA Membership number where applicable, vehicle and location details to the AAs Emergency Call Centre to provide you with necessary roadside assistance and medical evacuation. As an AA Member, you will get instant access to your Membership benefits, and if you are not an AA Member well provide emergency assistance at market related rates.

AA Accident Assist: his mobile application provides invaluable assistance at the scene of an accident, guiding you through the accident process, and recording all the information, photos, recordings and videos you will need for an insurance claim, or for later legal action, so that nothing is forgotten. Your incident will be saved to your website profile, so you can update the information at any time... and send the details directly to your insurer.

Do not find yourself in a critical situation without the expert help and guidance you need get the power of the AA in your pocket with the AA Rescue Service app suite. Remember, anyone can register and download the app, so make sure your friends and family are registered too, at www.aaaccident.co.za.

If you would like to become an AA Member to get all the help you need in a roadside emergency, visit www.aa.co.za or call us on 0861 000 234, weekdays between 8am and 5pm.
